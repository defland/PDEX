About pandadex

Holding MoonDEX Masternode is a fantastic way to earn a passive income while providing support to the exchange. Not only the mining rewards, a significant portion of the exchange’s profits will be shared with the masternode hodlers who also provide additional access points to boost the speed. This is the unique feature of the MoonDEX and one of the reasons why we will be much faster than our competitors.
